Chef

Key Responsibilities:

1. Preparing and Cooking Food
- Menu Execution: Prepares dishes as per the menu, ensuring each item matches the established recipe and standards.
- Adaptability: Handles variations in customer orders, such as dietary restrictions or preferences.
- Efficiency: Works quickly during peak hours to serve customers without compromising quality.

2. Ensuring Food Quality and Hygiene
- Freshness: Selects and uses high-quality, fresh ingredients to maintain food standards.
- Hygiene Compliance: Adheres to strict food safety regulations, including proper storage, cooking, and serving temperatures.
- Presentation: Focuses on the visual appeal of each dish, even in the fast-paced environment of a food truck.

3. Innovating Recipes
- Creative Development: Experiments with new recipes or variations to keep the menu exciting and appealing.
- Customer Feedback: Incorporates suggestions from customers to refine dishes or create specials.
- Seasonal Menus: Introduces items based on seasonal ingredients or trends to keep offerings fresh.

Impact on the Food Truck:

- Brand Identity: A chef's unique style and creativity can define the food truck's theme and attract loyal customers.
- Revenue Growth: High-quality, innovative dishes can boost repeat business and word-of-mouth referrals.
- Customer Experience: A skilled chef enhances the overall dining experience, turning casual visitors into fans.

Salary: RM3000/month
